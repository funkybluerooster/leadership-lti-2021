Navigating Ministry in a Post-Pandemic Era Leadership Training Institute Virtual Conference June 13-15, 2021

Welcome to Leadership Training Institute, is a leadership training conference for clergy, laity and youth in the Eighth Episcopal District looking to learn from the leaders in the CME Church. For the second time since the beginning, we are excited that LTI will be virtually. Leadership Training Institute a virtual experience.

Not sure if LTI is right for you and your family?

Hear from the Dean of Leadership Training Institute, Rev. Dr. Clarence Kelby Heath of Carter Metropolitan CME Church as he will share why he believes your family will benefit from this virtual experience.

  • Prepare Congregations for Life Moving Forward
  • Provide Time to Understand about Physical and Mental Health During COVID
  • Further Learning about Technology and Social Media
  • Approaches for Church Ministry and Personal Readiness
  • Personal Development Emphasis for Children/Youth
  • Familiarize Ourselves with C.Y.Y.A.C Protocol

What is Leadership Training Institute?
Leadership Training Institute is one of the longest running training institutes in the Christian Methodist Church and we want to help you keep the tradition going. If you are in a member or a leader in a church, you cannot afford not to miss this virtual conference and learning sessions.
How do I register?
You can register by going to this link and clicking the "Register Now" link on the 8th episcopal district website.
